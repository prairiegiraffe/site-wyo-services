Careers

Work With WYO Services

We're a growing oilfield contractor based in the Powder River Basin. If you know your way around heavy equipment and hard work, we want to hear from you.

Join the Crew

WYO Services Is Hiring

WYO Services has been growing since 2012, and our project load continues to expand across the Powder River Basin. We're always looking for experienced operators, equipment hands, and field crew who can handle the demands of oilfield site work in Wyoming.

We don't have a long list of open positions posted here right now. That's not how we've typically hired. Most of our crew came through word of mouth -- the same way we've built our client base. But as the company grows, we want to make sure capable people can find us.

If you have experience running equipment, building fence, doing dirt work, or reclamation -- and you're willing to put in honest days in the field -- reach out. Our work is based out of Gillette and Douglas, Wyoming.

The Work

What Working at WYO Services Looks Like

Real Field Work

This is hands-on, outdoor work. You'll be running equipment, building pads, setting fence, and restoring land across active oil and gas sites. The work is physical, the conditions are Wyoming weather, and every day is different.

Powder River Basin

Our projects are in northeastern Wyoming -- open prairie, big skies, and working landscapes. If you live in or near Gillette or Douglas, your commute to the job site stays reasonable. No out-of-state travel.

Growing Company

WYO Services started as a small fencing crew in 2012 and now runs seven service lines. The company is still growing, which means opportunity for people who show up and do the work.

Small Crew, Direct Communication

This isn't a corporate operation with layers of management. You'll work directly with experienced people, and the owners are on the ground. Decisions get made in the field, not in an office three states away.

How to Apply

Get in Touch

We keep it simple. If you're interested in working with WYO Services, give us a call or send a message through our contact form. Tell us about your experience, what equipment you can run, and where you're located.

We're particularly interested in people with experience in heavy equipment operation, dirt work, fencing, reclamation, and general oilfield site construction. A CDL is a plus. Willingness to work hard in all conditions is a requirement.
