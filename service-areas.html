Serving the Powder River Basin

WYO Services operates primarily out of Gillette and Douglas, Wyoming — with crews and equipment positioned for fast mobilization across the basin's active oil and gas regions.

Where We Work

Two Regions. Full Basin Coverage.

WYO Services is headquartered in Gillette, Wyoming with additional operations in Douglas. These two locations give us coverage across the Powder River Basin's most active oil and gas regions — and let us mobilize equipment and crews to job sites fast.

Our local presence means shorter mobilization times, lower transport costs, and crews who already know the terrain, the weather, and the conditions that come with working in northeastern Wyoming.
