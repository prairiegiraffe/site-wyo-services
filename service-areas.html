Service Areas

Serving the Powder River Basin

WYO Services operates primarily out of Gillette and Douglas, Wyoming — with crews and equipment positioned for fast mobilization across the basin's active oil and gas regions.

Headquarters

Gillette, Wyoming

Gillette is home base for WYO Services — our office at 410 Limestone Ave is where projects get scoped, equipment gets dispatched, and crews stage before heading to job sites across Campbell County and the northern Powder River Basin.

The Gillette region is the heart of Wyoming's oil and gas activity. WYO Services has worked the terrain here since 2012 — we know the soil conditions, the access challenges, and the landowners. That familiarity translates to faster mobilization, fewer surprises, and projects that stay on schedule.

From this hub we serve pad construction, reclamation, fencing, cattle guards, infrastructure, and full turnkey site development across Campbell, Crook, Weston, and Johnson counties.

Southern Basin Operations

Douglas, Wyoming

Douglas extends WYO Services' reach into the southern Powder River Basin. With active crews and equipment positioned in Converse County, we cover the growing oil and gas activity south of Gillette without the long mobilization hauls.

The Douglas region includes active drilling and production zones across Converse and Natrona counties. WYO Services runs the same full scope of services here — pad construction, dirt work, reclamation, fencing, and infrastructure — with crews who know the local terrain and conditions.

Having a second operating base means operators in the southern basin get the same fast response and local knowledge that our Gillette clients have relied on for over a decade.
