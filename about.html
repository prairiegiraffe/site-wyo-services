About WYO Services

Built From the Ground Up

From a small fencing crew to a full-scope oilfield contractor — WYO Services grew the way everything grows in Wyoming: through hard work, strong relationships, and showing up when it matters.

Our Story

Started with Fence Posts. Grew with the Basin.

WYO Services was founded in 2012 by Kyle and Jane Materi in Gillette, Wyoming. What started as a small fencing operation quickly grew as clients asked for more. One job led to dirt work, then reclamation, then full site development — and the company expanded to match the demand.

Within a few years, WYO Services had evolved from setting fence posts to running heavy equipment across multiple oilfield sites in the Powder River Basin. The growth wasn't planned from a boardroom — it happened in the field, one handshake and one completed project at a time.

Today, WYO Services operates out of both Gillette and Douglas, delivering seven core services that cover the full lifecycle of an oilfield site. The company's reputation has been built almost entirely through word-of-mouth — field managers and project leads who've worked with WYO once keep calling back.

Leadership

Kyle & Jane Materi

Kyle and Jane Materi bring ranching roots and field experience to every project WYO Services takes on. Kyle's background in hands-on land work and heavy equipment operation shaped the company's approach: show up, do the job right, and build relationships that last longer than any single contract.

Jane's role in operations and business management has been central to scaling the company from a two-person crew to a multi-service contractor with the capacity to support large-scale oil and gas projects across the basin.
